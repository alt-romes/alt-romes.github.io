Welcome to Romes' Musings

I'm a student at Faculdade de Ciências e Tecnologia in Lisbon, currently writing my Master thesis on type-checking linear types in the Glasgow Haskell Compiler's Core intermediate language. I also work as part of the GHC Team at Well-Typed.

I try to write interdisciplinary articles, am a Haskell enthusiast and GHC contributor. Besides, my thesis and work, I'm developing a shader-centric and type-heavy 3d renderer / game engine, trying to get e-graphs into GHC, and learning Music Theory. You can find below my latests posts.
